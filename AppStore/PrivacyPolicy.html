Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

BlastPop is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services.

1.1 User-Generated Content

  • Mood Bubbles: Text entries you write in your daily mood bubbles are stored locally on your device.
  • Photo Collages: Photos you import and edit within the app are stored locally and are never uploaded to external servers.
  • Challenge Progress: Your challenge completion records and streak data are stored locally.

1.2 Usage Data

  • App Usage: We track basic app usage (total bubbles popped, time spent, features used) locally on your device to improve user experience.
  • Achievement Progress: Your badges and achievements are stored locally on your device.

1.3 Device Data

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash logs may be collected to help us fix bugs and improve app stability.
  • Device Token: If you enable push notifications, a device token may be collected for notification delivery purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is processed as follows:

  • Local Storage: All user content (mood bubbles, photos, challenge progress) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal data on external servers.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.
  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregate, anonymized usage data may be used to improve the app experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Local-Only Storage: All sensitive user data is stored locally on your device, not on external servers.
  • Secure Deletion: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app. All local data will be permanently removed.
  • No Account Required: BlastPop does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.
  • Photo Privacy: Photos imported for bubble collages remain on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

4. In-App Purchases

BlastPop offers premium subscriptions through the App Store:

  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We never collect your payment information.
  • Purchase History: Your subscription status is managed by Apple and stored locally on your device.
  • Restoration: You can restore purchases using the "Restore Purchase" feature in the app.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications:

  • Notification Content: We may send you notifications about daily challenges, streak reminders, and achievement unlocks.
  • Opt-Out: You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app settings.
  • No Profiling: Notifications are not used for advertising or user profiling.

6. Children's Privacy

BlastPop is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all data stored locally by opening the app.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data.
  • Export: You can export your mood records from the Profile section.
  • Privacy Controls: You can manage all privacy settings within the app's Settings screen.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
